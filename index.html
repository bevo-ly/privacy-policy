Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Bevo Creator (creator.bevo.ly) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our website and services (the “Services”). By using the Services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are & contact

Bevo Creator (“we,” “us,” “our”). For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@creator.bevo.ly.

2. Information we collect

  • Account & profile data such as name, display name, email address, and any details you provide when creating an account or contacting support.
  • Third-party login data (if you sign in with Facebook or similar), such as your public profile, name, and email address, as permitted by that platform and your settings.
  • Usage data including IP address, device identifiers, browser type, pages viewed, referring/exit pages, timestamps, and general location based on IP.
  • Transactional data if you purchase a plan, processed by our payment providers (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Cookies & similar technologies used to remember preferences, maintain sessions, and analyze usage.

3. How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services.
  • Authenticate users and maintain security.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms.
  • Analyze aggregate usage to improve performance and user experience.

4. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

We process personal data only where we have a legal basis, including: performance of a contract (to provide the Services), legitimate interests (to secure and improve the Services), consent (where required), and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Sharing of information

  • Service providers who help us host, operate, analyze, support, or process payments. They access data only to perform services for us and under contract.
  • Compliance and safety when we must respond to lawful requests or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers in the event of a merger, acquisition, or similar transaction, consistent with applicable law.
  • We do not sell personal data.

6. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in and understand how the Services are used. You can manage cookies in your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy (e.g., for the life of your account) and to comply with legal obligations. We then delete or anonymize data.

8. Data security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards to protect personal data during transfers.

11. Your rights

Subject to local law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data; object to or restrict processing; and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise these rights, email privacy@creator.bevo.ly.

12. Facebook data & deletion

If you sign in with Facebook or otherwise connect a Facebook account, we may receive your public profile and email, as permitted by Facebook and your settings. You can request deletion of Facebook-related data by emailing privacy@creator.bevo.ly with the subject “Facebook Data Deletion” and the email tied to your account. If we provide a status page, you can also check deletion status at /data-deletion-status on our site.

13. Changes to this policy

We may modify this Policy from time to time. We will post updates here and adjust the “Effective date” above. Material changes may be communicated via email or in-app notice.

14. Contact us

Email: privacy@creator.bevo.ly
